About

ServerlessDays Atlanta is a developer-oriented conference about serverless technologies.

We are platform and technology agnostic and want to create a space where we can all learn new things about serverless! This is our first year hosting ServerlessDays in Atlanta, GA!

We believe in and encourage practical sessions, in which developers share their experience and lessons from real-world projects.

ServerlessDays Atlanta is part of ServerlessDays (formerly JeffConf), a global series of events around the world fostering communities around serverless technologies.

We follow and enforce the Serverless Days Code of Conduct.
